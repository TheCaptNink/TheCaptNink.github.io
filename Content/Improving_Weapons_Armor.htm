Improving Weapons and Armor

The Blacksmith in the Hamlet is where you improve the weapons and armor of your heroes. Improving the Blacksmith unlocks increased improvements and reductions in improvement costs.

Remember to save your game often!

To Improve Weapons or Armor

  1. From the Hamlet, click the Blacksmith A bearded dude who improves your stuff. shop.
  2. Drag the potrait of the Hero whose items you want to improve into the Blacksmith area.
  4. Do one of the following:
    • To improve the Hero's weapons, click the Weapons button. The weapon is upgraded to the next level and the cost is deducted from your gold.
    • To improve the Hero's armor, click the Armor button. The armor is upgraded to the next level and the cost is deducted from your gold.
  5. When finished improving you weapons or armor, click Exit.

To Upgrade the Blacksmith

  1. From the Hamlet, click the Blacksmith A bearded dude who improves your stuff. shop.
  2. Drag the potrait of the Hero whose items you want to improve into the Blacksmith area.
  4. Do one of the following:
    • To improve the Hero's weapons, click the Weapons button. The weapon is upgraded to the next level and the cost is deducted from your gold.
    • To improve the Hero's armor, click the Armor button. The armor is upgraded to the next level and the cost is deducted from your gold.
  5. When finished improving you weapons or armor, click Exit.
